What is ArcMap beyond a domain service?
ArcMap is the core social layer of the Arc Network. We are building a comprehensive social network where users can form relationships, interact, and build reputation, entirely decentralized and on-chain.
How can I send or receive funds?
With ArcMap, you can replace complex hex addresses with a simple, readable Arc name. This allows you to send and receive USDC and other assets instantly and safely within the network.
Can I share and trade NFTs?
Yes! Your ArcMap profile acts as your digital gallery. You can showcase your personal NFTs to the community and even list them on the direct, built-in marketplace right from your profile.
How do I integrate Arc names to my app?
We provide robust developer docs and SDKs. You can easily integrate our resolver contracts to display human-readable names instead of raw addresses in your DApp.
